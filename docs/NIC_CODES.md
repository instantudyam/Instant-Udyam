## ✅ How to Choose Correct NIC Code

### Step 1: Identify Primary Activity
What is your **main business activity**?

### Step 2: Match with Table
Find your business type in the tables above

### Step 3: Verify on Portal
1. Go to [udyamregistration.gov.in](https://udyamregistration.gov.in/)
2. Use NIC Code Finder
3. Enter your business type
4. Confirm the suggested code

### Step 4: Double-Check
- Does the description match your business?
- Is this your main activity?
- Are secondary activities mentioned?

---

## 🎯 What If You Have Multiple Activities?

**Example**: Bookstore + Online Sales + Cafe

✅ **Choose PRIMARY activity** (highest revenue/focus)
- In this case: Bookstore (4762)

✅ **Mention SECONDARY activities** in description
- Online sales (4791)
- Cafe operations (5610)

⚠️ **Only ONE primary NIC code per registration**

## 🔄 Can You Change NIC Code?

| Timing | Can Change? | How? |
|--------|-------------|------|
| Before submission | ✅ Yes | Choose correct one |
| Within 30 days | ✅ Yes | Update in portal |
| After 30 days | ❌ No | Need reregistration |

**Pro Tip**: Get it right the first time!

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

### ❌ Mistake 1: Wrong Code
**Impact**: May affect loan eligibility and schemes  
**Solution**: Use NIC finder, not manual entry

### ❌ Mistake 2: Too Broad Code
**Impact**: Schemes may not apply  
**Solution**: Be specific about your business

### ❌ Mistake 3: Not Updated Later
**Impact**: Scheme eligibility issues  
**Solution**: Update within 30 days if incorrect
