## ✅ What is Udyam Registration?

**Udyam** is India's government system to officially recognize Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs). It replaces the old MSME registration system and provides:

- 💰 **Access to Collateral-Free Loans** (up to ₹1 crore)
- 📊 **Government Subsidies & Grants**
- 🏦 **Priority Sector Lending Benefits**
- 📋 **Eligibility for Government Schemes**
- 🎯 **Priority in Government Tenders**
